Our Services

Services we are providing to resolve all your industrial issues. Click on services to know more about them.

Factories Act

Maintenance and submission of the statutory Records / Returns under Factories act, 1948.

E.S.I Act

We help new establishment to get ESI registration code number within prescribed time as per Act.

P.F Act

We are managing PF records with numbers of clients and helping them in any type of PF issue raised.

Gratuity Act

Registration and Maintenance under Gratuity Act, 1972.

Shop and Commercial Act

Registration and Maintenance under Shop and Commercial Act with respect to different states.

Standing Orders

Registration under Standing Orders Act, 1946 and maintaining your Standing Order Act.

Registration under Factories Act

Registration and obtaining Factory Licence from the Department for your organisation.

S.S.I Act

Maintenance of all the documents and data of Small Scale Industries including its registration and other legal work.

Pollution

Registration under all three Air, Water and Environment Act and maintenance of all documents.
